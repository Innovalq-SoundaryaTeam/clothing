Top 10 Summer Fashion Trends

A simple boutique style guide for floral dresses, clean shirts, accessories, and summer-ready outfits.

May 21, 2026 Rose Studio Trends

Fresh and simple summer styling

Summer fashion should feel light, comfortable, and easy to style. Floral dresses, cotton shirts, sneakers, and soft accessories are perfect for creating a clean boutique look.

Choose breathable fabrics, minimal colors, and simple silhouettes. Pair floral outfits with neutral bags or sneakers for a balanced everyday style.

1. Floral dresses

Floral dresses are one of the easiest ways to create a fresh summer outfit. They work well for brunch, vacations, casual outings, and family events.

2. Minimal shirts

For men, a clean white or linen shirt gives a premium look without too much effort. It can be paired with chinos, trousers, denim, or blazers.

3. Accessories

Accessories like handbags, watches, and sneakers complete the outfit. Keep the colors simple and match them with the overall outfit tone.
