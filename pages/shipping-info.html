Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery times, shipping charges, and our returns and exchange policy.

Free Shipping

Free standard shipping on all orders above ₹2,999. Orders below this amount ship with a flat delivery charge shown at checkout.

Delivery Time

Orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours and delivered in 2–4 business days across most serviceable pin codes.

Easy Returns

Free returns and exchanges within 14 days of delivery, as long as the item is unused and has its original tags.

Shipping Policy

We currently ship across India. All orders are processed and packed with care at our Coimbatore boutique before handover to our courier partners.

  • Orders are dispatched within 24–48 hours of confirmation.
  • Standard delivery takes 2–4 business days depending on your location.
  • Free shipping applies automatically on orders above ₹2,999.
  • A tracking link is shared by email/SMS once your order ships.

Returns & Exchanges

Not the right fit or style? We make returns simple.

  • Returns and exchanges are accepted within 14 days of delivery.
  • Items must be unused, unwashed, and returned with original tags and packaging.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of pickup.
  • To start a return or exchange, reach out from our Contact page with your order ID.
